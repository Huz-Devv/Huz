Fam Chat Bot - Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • User Data: We collect Discord usernames, user IDs, and messages sent to or through the Bot.
  • Server Data: We collect server-specific information such as channel IDs and server settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain the functionalities of the Bot.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the Bot's performance and features.
  • Moderation: To enforce server rules and prevent abuse.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We don't retain your Data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, or delete your personal data. You can exercise these rights by contacting the Server administrators.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Server. Your continued use of the Bot after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Server administrators.
